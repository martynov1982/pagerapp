Privacy

No phone number is ever exposed. Contact happens through an ID you control.

Contextual identity

Different relationships see a different name, avatar and description — not a different account.

Directional access

Text, audio and video are granted individually, by the receiver, per relationship.

Safer first contact

Nobody enters a conversation without a request being accepted first.

Temporary access

Couriers, services and short-term contacts can be granted access that expires on its own.

One identity, not many

A single Pager ID underlies every profile — nothing about multiprofile fragments the account.
